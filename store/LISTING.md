## Full description (≤4000 chars)

Timestamp Screen Recorder records your Android screen and stamps the exact
date and time — down to the millisecond — directly into the video.

Most screen recorders just capture the screen. This one adds a precise,
always-visible clock (yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss.SSS) that is burned into the
recording across every app, so the timing is part of the footage itself — not
metadata you have to trust.

WHO IT'S FOR
• QA & mobile engineers measuring UI latency or proving when a bug happened
• App reviewers and benchmarkers comparing response times
• Anyone who needs time-stamped, tamper-evident screen proof
• Speedrunners, traders, and lab/field recording

FEATURES
• Millisecond timestamp overlay captured into the video
• Multiple formats: 24-hour, 12-hour, time-only, or Unix epoch (ms)
• Optional UTC time and an elapsed-time counter — ideal for log correlation
• Optional microphone narration recorded into the video
• Choose the timestamp position (six placements) and font size
• Low-resolution modes (30% / 50% / 75% / 100%) to keep files small
• Adjustable bitrate
• Saves MP4 to your Movies folder — no watermark, no time limit
• One-tap Stop from the notification that returns you to the app
• Light & dark theme, follows your system setting
• 8 languages: English, Tiếng Việt, Français, 日本語, ไทย, 中文, Español, Deutsch

PRIVACY
• No ads, no account, no tracking, no internet access
• Recordings stay on your device unless you share them yourself

The screen-capture consent dialog appears each time you start, and the
timestamp overlay needs the "Display over other apps" permission.
